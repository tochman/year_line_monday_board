# YearLine - Timeline Visualization for monday.com

## See Your monday.com Board Data on a Timeline

**YearLine** brings simple, powerful Gantt visualization to your monday.com boards. Whether you're managing a product launch, coordinating a team, or tracking multiple projects, YearLine turns your board data into **clear, interactive timelines**.

Stop juggling spreadsheets and static views. With YearLine, your monday.com workspace becomes a **dynamic visual command center** where you can see the big picture and drill into the details—all without leaving monday.com.

---

## Key Features

### **Flexible Visualization**
Display your tasks organized by **groups**, **status labels**, or **assigned teammates**. Switch between views instantly to see your projects from different angles. Get the perspective you need, when you need it.

### **Drag-and-Drop Editing**
Adjust timelines on the fly. *Drag tasks to new dates* or *resize them to change durations*. Your changes sync directly back to your monday.com board in real-time. **No extra steps. No manual updates.**

### **No Setup Required**
YearLine works with your existing monday.com boards. **No migration, no duplicate data entry.** Just connect and visualize. Your timeline columns and date fields are automatically detected and ready to use.

### **Smart Timeline Views**
- **Group View**: See all your board groups laid out chronologically
- **Status View**: Track progress by status categories across your timeline
- **People View**: Understand workload distribution and team capacity at a glance

### **Export & Share**
Generate timeline snapshots for stakeholder updates, client presentations, or team reviews. Keep everyone aligned on project progress.
